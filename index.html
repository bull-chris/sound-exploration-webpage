Sound Compass - Microbit Piano

Sound compass is a musical instrument made using microbit. Microbit is a small circuit board with multiple sensors in it. For this instrument, we are using a the built-in compass sensor within the microbit. when the user rotates this board at a certain angle it produces piano sounds. there are 8 pre-defined sounds for the 8 different angles at each cardinal and ordinal direction.

You can download the makecode program here: https://github.com/vanathygowreesan/iot-sound--compass.git

Instructions to Play

  1. Step 1: Open Chrome Browser and open this link.
  2. Step 2: Connect your microbit to your PC/Mac using a micro USB cable.
  3. Step 3: Pair your microbit with a chrome browser.
  4. Step 4: Rotate your microbit, when you rotate your microbit at certain angle you will hear a piano sound/
